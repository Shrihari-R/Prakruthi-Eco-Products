About

Prakruthi Eco products is a startup started by young group of Entrepreneurs who are committed towards the reducing the impact adverse impact on mother earth by promoting eco friendly, bio degradable products. We are the manufacturers of areca plates, bowls, cutleries which are not only sustainable but also very beautiful.

Vision

We have a vision of utilizing the naturally available products and creating the value added products out of it without disturbing our eco system, there by generating Business and employment in semi-urban and rural areas. This not only adds to the economy of our country but also controls the extent of migrating population to cities and thereby reducing the gap between urban and semi urban areas.
